Vission

To bring the naxal- affected, tribal and poor youth of the area into the main stream of the national power through a continuous self and external quality evaluation.

Mission

• To create specific academic programs.

• To create the academic environment to get the best results in teaching- learning process.

• To encourage self evaluation and innovations in education.

• To conduct and participate in quality-related training programs for the promotion of teaching-learning process.

• To collaborate with stakeholders of the college for quality evaluation, promotion and sustenance.
